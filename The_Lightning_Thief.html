The Lightning Thief

"Percy Jackson and the Olympians" is a captivating novel series written by American author Rick Riordan. The series follows the adventures of Percy Jackson, a young demigod who discovers his extraordinary heritage as the son of Poseidon, the Greek god of the sea.

The novels have gained immense popularity among readers of all ages, thanks to Riordan's engaging storytelling and the fascinating blend of Greek mythology and modern-day settings. The series takes place in a world where the Greek gods exist in the 21st century and explores Percy's journey as he navigates the challenges of being a demigod.

The success of the book series led to the creation of two movies based on the first two novels: "Percy Jackson & the Olympians: The Lightning Thief" (2010) and "Percy Jackson: Sea of Monsters" (2013).

As Percy navigates the world of gods and monsters, he embarks on a quest to prevent a war among the gods by recovering Zeus' stolen lightning bolt. Along the way, Percy encounters mythical creatures, forms friendships with other demigods, and uncovers shocking truths about his own identity.
