The Sea Of Monsters

"The Sea of Monsters" is the second book in the "Percy Jackson & the Olympians" series written by Rick Riordan. The story continues to follow the adventures of Percy Jackson, a young demigod and the son of Poseidon, the Greek god of the sea.

In this thrilling installment, Percy discovers that his safe haven, Camp Half-Blood, is in danger. The magical borders of the camp are failing, and the only way to restore its protection is by retrieving the legendary Golden Fleece. This powerful artifact has the ability to heal and protect, and it is located in the treacherous Sea of Monsters, also known as the Bermuda Triangle.

Joined by his loyal friends Annabeth and Grover, Percy embarks on a perilous quest to find the Golden Fleece and save Camp Half-Blood. Along the way, they face numerous challenges, including deadly monsters, mythical creatures, and the machinations of the Titan lord Kronos, who seeks to destroy the gods and overthrow Olympus.

As they navigate through the Sea of Monsters, Percy and his friends encounter various Greek mythological figures and legends. They must confront their fears, test their loyalties, and tap into their unique demigod abilities to overcome the obstacles in their path
